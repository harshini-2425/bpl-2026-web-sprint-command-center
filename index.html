Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the sprint

What is the BlackPanda Labs Sprint?

+

The BlackPanda Labs Sprint is a 48-hour intensive building event where developers, designers, and creators collaborate to build innovative projects. You can choose from 6 different tracks and work solo or as a team.

Who can participate?

+

Anyone with passion for building can participate! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, there's a track and challenge for you. We welcome students, professionals, and enthusiasts from all skill levels.

How do I form a team?

+

You can register solo or with a team of up to 5 members. Use the registration form to create or join a team. Teams are finalized 48 hours before the sprint begins, so get your group together early!

What resources are provided?

+

We provide starter kits, API documentation, design templates, and access to mentor support throughout the sprint. All resources are shared in our Discord community and documentation portal.

How are winners decided?

+

Winners are judged based on innovation, functionality, design, and presentation. Each track has its own judges and winners. All submissions are evaluated fairly by industry experts.

What are the prizes?

+

Top projects in each track receive cash prizes, exclusive merchandise, mentorship opportunities, and potential internship offers from partner companies. Every participant also gets a certificate of participation.
